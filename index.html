Frequently Asked Questions

Questions before you schedule?

Call or text Al’s at (260) 348-5609 when the job needs a custom answer.

Do you provide mobile detailing?

Yes. Mobile service is available by appointment, subject to location access, weather, scheduling and service type.

Do you clean dealership inventory?

Yes. Services can include inventory washdowns, interior refreshes, photo-ready preparation and recurring programs.

Can you clean a large number of staged vehicles?

Yes. Fleet, auction, rental and staged-vehicle work is quoted by count, condition, service level, location and turnaround.

How is pricing determined?

Personal packages have starting prices. Final pricing can change based on size, condition, pet hair, staining, buildup and add-ons. Business work is custom quoted.

Do you service areas outside Fort Wayne?

Nearby areas may be available by appointment. Travel distance and job size may affect availability and price.

Can dealerships schedule recurring service?

Recurring service may be available after the scope, vehicle volume and schedule are confirmed.

What should I include in a quote request?

Include vehicle type, count, location, desired service, condition concerns and preferred date. Photos can help.
