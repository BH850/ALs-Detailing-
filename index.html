Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions before booking.

These FAQs help both personal customers and business clients understand the service better.

Do you provide mobile detailing?

Yes. Al’s Detailing is designed as a mobile service and can travel to the vehicle or job location.

Do you clean dealership inventory?

Yes. Dealership rows, used-car lots, staged inventory, and recurring lot work are part of the service model.

Can you service multiple vehicles at one time?

Yes. Bulk vehicle requests, fleet jobs, staged vehicle groups, and recurring programs can be quoted separately.

How is pricing determined?

Personal vehicles can use tiered package pricing, while dealerships, fleets, and bulk projects are usually quote-based.

Do you service areas outside Fort Wayne?

Yes, nearby surrounding communities can be serviced depending on the job size and schedule.

Can you help with resale or inventory presentation?

Yes. That is one of the strongest use cases for the business — helping vehicles look cleaner and more presentable.
