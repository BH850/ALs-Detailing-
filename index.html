Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions before booking.

Do you provide mobile detailing?

Yes. Al’s Detailing is designed as a mobile service and can travel to the vehicle or job location.

Do you clean dealership inventory?

Yes. Dealership rows, used car lots, staged inventory, and recurring lot work are part of the service model.

Can you service multiple vehicles at one time?

Yes. High-volume vehicle requests, fleet jobs, staged vehicle groups, and recurring programs can be quoted separately.

How is pricing determined?

Personal vehicles are priced according to the selected detailing package, vehicle size, condition, and requested add-ons. Dealership, fleet, and high-volume services are priced through customized quotes.

Do you service areas outside Fort Wayne?

Nearby communities may be serviced depending on the job size, travel distance, and scheduling availability.

Can you prepare vehicles for resale or customer presentation?

Yes. Al’s Detailing helps personal vehicles, dealership inventory, rental vehicles, and fleet units look cleaner and more presentable for resale, photography, delivery, or customer viewing.
